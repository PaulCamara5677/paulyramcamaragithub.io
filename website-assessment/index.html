Programmes...

Our wide range of photography and videography learning and working programs, which include workshops, internships, and apprenticeships, are designed to develop in aspiring photographers and videographers both technical proficiency and creative vision.

Videography genres...

A vast variety of genres are included in the discipline of videography; they include more relaxed forms like vlogging, trip videos, and social media content, as well as more formal ones like corporate videos, advertising, and documentary filmmaking. Whether their goal is to inform, convince, or just to entertain, this diversity enables creators to express themselves in a unique way.

Camera History...

The history of cameras spans from the early 1800s, with cumbersome daguerreotypes used mainly for portraits, to the 20th century's portable 35mm film cameras capturing pivotal historical events. The late 20th century saw the rise of digital cameras, revolutionizing image quality and accessibility. Today, advanced smartphone cameras make high-quality photography and videography accessible to everyone.
